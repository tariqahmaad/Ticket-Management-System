Ticket Management System

The Application interface has been integrated with various of operation to bring easier while using this application. we ensure security of information and management of tickets

This Appplication would be used by multiple users who would want to access to different data and informations. Those roles includes Passengers, Security personnel and flight staff.


They can easily verify the names and information of the passengers travelling across a series of flight manifests.

They can clear passengers for their flight.



They can access information about passengers travelling in their respective flights.

Before the flight, they generate a report of all checked in passengers who have cleared security. The report would contain details of number of passengers and their names.



They can use their unique PNR to access flight information and perform self check in.

They can also search and book flights.
